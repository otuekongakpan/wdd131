Bucket List

Here are some must-see attractions and experiences to add to your Nigeria bucket list:

Visit the Olumo Rock

Olumo Rock is a natural monolith located in Ogun State, Nigeria. It is often referred to as the Fortress for the Egba People.

Experience the Calabar Carnival

The Calabar Carnival is an annual event held in Calabar, Cross River State, Nigeria.

Explore the Aso Rock

Aso Rock is a large rock formation located on the outskirts of Abuja, Nigeria's capital city.

Relax at Obudu Mountain Resort

Obudu Mountain Resort, also known as Obudu Cattle Ranch, is a popular tourist destination located in Cross River State, Nigeria.

See the Iconic Zuma Rock

Zuma Rock is a large monolithic rock formation located in Niger State, Nigeria. It is often referred to as the Gateway to Abuja.

Walk the Canopy at Lekki Conservation Center

The Lekki Conservation Center is a nature reserve located in Lagos, Nigeria. It is known for its diverse flora and fauna, as well as its canopy walkway.

Visit the Olumirin Waterfall

Olumirin Waterfall, also known as Erin-Ijesha Waterfall, is a stunning natural attraction located in Osun State, Nigeria.
